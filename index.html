Ben Glenn

About Ben

Ben Glenn, the Simple ADHD Expert®, aka The Chalkguy, was diagnosed with Dyslexia and other Learning Disabilities while in grade school. As an adult he discovered that he had suffered from ADHD all along and suddenly everything about his life made perfect sense. He left college to pursue a love of art and speaking, and has been “on the road”, full-time since 1995, traveling to every part of the United States as well as to more remote places like China and Australia to share his message. Ever since his ADD diagnosis, he has been on a personal quest to learn as much as possible about his “disorder”. This journey has completely changed the way he views himself and ADHD. Nowhere is the saying “Knowledge is Power” more applicable than when it comes to ADD.




Portfolio

In 2015 my daughter, who has a lot of my same struggles, was begining to have a difficult time in school. The typical studying and repetition were not producing positive results. I got creative and developed several apps to help her. Using the authoring tool GameSalad, I was able to create and publish my first app, Alien Math Innovation. Soon after came, Coin Crusher, Doomsday Division, The Rainbow Rider, Booger Ball, and a spelling app unique for just her school. These apps were extremely helpful to my daughter and sparked my interest. 5 years later, with the help of COVID, I find myself back in school attending Eleven Fifty Acadamy with hopes of becoming a full-stack developer. The following is my portfolio of work from my time at Eleven Fifty. Please feel free to review and reach out to me with any questions. Thanks for taking the time to explore my body of work.

A Little More From Ben...

I struggled with Attention Deficit Disorder (ADHD) throughout my formative years, ultimately finding it impossible to stay interested in school and leaving following my junior year in college. Although my ADHD caused me to struggle academically, it proved to be a perfect companion in my career as a performer, Speaker, artist, and educator. Since 1995 I have spoken to thousands of people, written 5 books, and published 7 educational apps. It was while making my apps, such as Alien Math Innovation, Coin Crusher, Doomsday Division, and more, that I gain a greater interest in coding, UX/UI, product development, and distribution. All of the games I personally created were developed using the authoring tool GameSalad. I was also the art director for the game Goofy Gophers. My hope is to become a full-stack developer, and with attending Eleven Fifty Acadamy I am on my way. Below you will find, my core values, developer skillset, along with my other skills.
